BMI Calculator

BMI is a simple calculation that provides a general idea of whether someone is within a healthy weight range, you divide your weight (in kilograms) by your height (in centimeters) squared. BMI is used to classify adults into weight categories: underweight (BMI less than 18.5), normal weight (BMI between 18.5 and 24.9), overweight (BMI between 25 and 29.9), and obese (BMI of 30 or higher). While useful, BMI doesn't distinguish between muscle mass and fat mass. It may overestimate body fat in athletes and underestimate it in older adults.
